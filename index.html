About Tom

Tom Spyker is the son of two artists and has been creating art since he could hold a pencil (though he's been working consistently for about eight years).

He most often creates art using Procreate or with gouache paints, but loves to experiment and has worked with many mediums from wood carving to screen printing clothes. Tom loves to create sci-fi and fantasy-inspired artworks, along with some fan art for his favorite games and shows.

He is currently enrolled at the Cleveland Institute of Art where he is pursuing his dream of creating his own video games.
